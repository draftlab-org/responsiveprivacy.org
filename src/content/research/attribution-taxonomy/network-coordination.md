Recommended implementation:

1. Threat Intelligence Sharing:
   1. When activating privacy mode due to threat, notify partner organizations
   2. Provide threat assessment summary
   3. Recommend privacy level for partners to consider
2. Reciprocal Notification:
   1. Partners notify your organization when they activate privacy mode
   2. Facilitated by shared threat database/server
3. Coordinated Response:
   1. For network-wide threats, consider synchronized privacy level changes
   2. Shared restoration timing → if each holds shared threat model…
4. Automated Notification Mechanism:
   1. Secure channel for threat details
   2. Opt-in/opt-out for notifications
